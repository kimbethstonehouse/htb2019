Spenny

Fly to Financial Freedom


What is Spenny?

Spenny is an ambitious attempt to revolutionise personal financial budgeting, which combines statistical techniques and macroeconomic data to contextualise all of our decisions in the an economic climate.

How do we do this?

We use various data sources of typical household spending and prices in the UK for different regions and earnings levels to generate bespoke budgets for everyone that pertain to their situation. Then, you can set savings goals that Spenny will help you work towards.
